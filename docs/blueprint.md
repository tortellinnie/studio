# **App Name**: Ascension Profile

## Core Features:

- Projects Showcase: A dedicated section to visually display portfolio projects, complete with images, detailed descriptions, technologies used, and direct links to live demos and GitHub repositories.
- Interactive 'My Journey' Timeline: An engaging, chronological timeline presenting academic, professional, and significant personal growth milestones.
- Awards & Certifications Display: A consolidated hub to highlight all earned awards, academic honors, and professional certifications with relevant details and verification links.
- Leadership & Experience Overview: A clear section detailing leadership roles held, responsibilities, key contributions, and relevant work experience.
- About & Contact Section: A concise introductory 'About Me' statement and clear display of contact details and professional social media links.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary action color: A professional and modern blue, Hex: #2663D9, providing a sense of stability and innovation.
- Accent color: A vibrant cyan, Hex: #47D0EB, for highlights, interactive elements, and visual interest, analogous to the primary blue.
- Background: Clean and spacious white, Hex: #FFFFFF, to reinforce minimalism and ensure content clarity.
- Text and secondary elements: A deep, unobtrusive dark gray, Hex: #333333, for high readability and a sleek, professional aesthetic.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, objective, and highly readable characteristics across all content, supporting a clean, professional appeal.
- Utilize minimalist, professional line icons (preferably SVG) that complement the clean aesthetic and improve scannability without clutter.
- Implement a responsive, grid-based layout with generous white space around elements, ensuring clarity, hierarchical structure, and a premium, modern feel on all devices.
- Incorporate subtle, smooth transitions for page navigation and understated hover effects on interactive components, enhancing user experience without being distracting.
